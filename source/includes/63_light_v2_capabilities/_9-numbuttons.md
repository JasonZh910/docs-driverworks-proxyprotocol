## num buttons

Indicates the number of buttons the driver supports. The options are:

0 = None - Also the field in Composer Programming Commands box.

1 = Top

2 = Top and Bottom

3 = Top, Bottom, and Toggle


### Signature

`<num_buttons</num_buttons>`


### Example

```xml
<capabilities>
   <num_buttons>2</num_buttons>
</capabilities>
```
